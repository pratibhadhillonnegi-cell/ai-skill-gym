5 Prompt Mistakes That Kill Output Quality

Published: May 15, 2026

If your AI outputs feel "soulless" or "generic", you are probably making one of these five mistakes:

  • Mistake 1: Not defining the target audience.
  • Mistake 2: Asking for too many things in one prompt (chain them instead).
  • Mistake 3: Using adjectives instead of examples (e.g., "Make it funny" vs "Write it in the style of Jerry Seinfeld").
  • Mistake 4: Forgetting to specify the output format.
  • Mistake 5: Accepting the first output without iterating.
